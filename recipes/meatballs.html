Beer Flavored Meatballs

Description

Traditionally consisting of ground beef or veal (and sometimes pork), meatballs are meat shaped into small balls.

Ingredients

  • 300 g ground beef
  • 300 g sausage
  • 200 mg stale bread
  • 80 g flour
  • 3 onions
  • 3 glasses of beer
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 spoon of tomato concentrate
  • milk
  • butter
  • pepper
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • thyme
